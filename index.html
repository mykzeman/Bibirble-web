Game Overview

Guess the verse by selecting the correct book and entering the chapter:verse digits. Green means exact match, yellow means correct digit in the wrong place, gray means not in the answer.

Daily challenges refresh at UTC midnight. Random games are replayable by seed. Hard mode only accepts valid verses and forces clues to be respected.

How to play: Choose a book, then enter the four chapter/verse digits based on the clue.
Seed hint: Use the Settings screen to set or randomize a seed for replayable games.
